Minimalist, fencer, environmentalist, cat lover, coder

I'm a Bay Area native who loves to fence, code, and eat good food. I also love classic rock and Hamilton the musical. Not necessarily in that order.


I've worked on projects under UC Berkeley's Society of Women Engineers, and am proud to be part of the nation-wide Kode With Klossy and #BuiltByGirls programs. (If you're interested in joining either, shoot me an email for a referral link!)


As a student and second-generation immigrant in San Francisco, I am blessed with diverse interactions and fresh perspective. This contributes to my passion for the environment, advocacy for females in tech, and is even exhibited by my love for photography.
